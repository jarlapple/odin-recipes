Spanish Omelette

Description

An excellent dish that is also easy to prepare

Ingredients = Easy to find Skill level = Basic skills

Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2 - 3
  • Patatoes: 2 - 3
  • Olive oil
  • Cayenne Pepper Flakes
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs to a deep bowl and chirp them.
  2. Add pepper, salt and pepper flakes to the chirped egg bowl.
  3. Mash the patatoes.
  4. Start to heat t he pan and put some olive oil into the pan.
  5. Add the mashed patatoes into the pan.
  6. Wait until they get brown.
  7. Add the eggs
  8. Wait until the eggs are done.
  9. Its done.
